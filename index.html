How ApolloShield Works

Detects All Drone Types

AirFence detects drones using multiple technologies - cameras, audio and radio sensors - so no drone can evade detection.

Locates Drones & Their Operators

AirFence estimates the location of the detected drones and their operators, allowing your security team to assess the risk.

Collects Drone Forensics

AirFence finds the unique identifiers of most drones, allowing law enforcement agencies to hunt irresposible operators.

Commands Unwanted Drones To Land

AirFence takes control of supported drones and sends them a "go home" command, disconnecting the original operator and forcing them to land safely.
